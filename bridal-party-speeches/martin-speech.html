Martin

To Ejay & Swodei, Cannot express the joy and happiness that is flowing through me at the prospect of you two finally getting married. I've always admired your commitment to one another, the friendship you share and the outward message you tell without even realising. The two of you are definitely one of a kind. Jesus is truly the centre and when He is the centre, there is love

And this is my prayer: that your love may abound more and more in knowledge and depth insight, so that you may be able to discern what is best and may be pure and blameless for the day of Christ (Philippians 1:9-10 NIV)

I cannot go any deeper than scripture itself. And this interprets what I pray for the two of you. That you love, more and more. Know Christ, more and more. Gain depth of insight, more and more. Deeper into Father's presence as you unite in his name. Amen.

I love you both.

Martin Ojo
